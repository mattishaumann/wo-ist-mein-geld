Wo ist mein Geld?

Ein Status- und ETA-Tracker für jede Auszahlung. Faktischer Status, ein verständlicher Grund für jede Verzögerung, ein voraussichtliches Ankunftsdatum. Keine Anlageberatung, keine echten Zahlungen.

How this was made: full transparency

I vibecoded this prototype with AI in about three hours. My own time went into the part that actually matters: scraping the ~200 most recent Play-Store reviews, coding them into themes, and deliberately picking something users would actually be pleased with - not the most creative idea I could come up with but might not be of greatest value.

01 · Motivation & method
Scrape 197 reviews→ Cluster themes→ Score opportunities→ Vibecode dummy

I scraped and coded 197 recent Play-Store reviews (Apr–Jun 2026), clustered them into themes and scored each pain on severity, frequency, and if that suits a max 5-hour demo project that tackles something people complain about.

The way I see it, the loudest themes (login, crashes) are engineering reliability problems and fixes, nothing that really fits a product prototype. So I went for something where I feel design could be the bottleneck. After a payout is requested, the money just „disappears" for days. Reading the reviews, people aren't really asking for speed. They're asking for certainty, or transparency. The money is „in the air", neither on the Scalable account nor in their bank account.

03 · The idea

A status & ETA tracker for every payout: the steps the money passes through, a plain-language reason for any delay, an estimated arrival date, and a pre-filled support handoff if the ETA slips. Factual status only: no advice, no recommendations, no real payments.

Scalable can't make every payout instant overnight, but it can make every payout understandable immediately.

04 · How I'd measure it
Payout-related support contacts
Per 1,000 payouts, A/B vs. control, target −20–30 % after rollout.
„Warum dauert das?" opens
Sheet open rate per active payout, adoption & self-service signal.
Post-payout confidence
1-tap in-app survey after arrival, target +0.5 on a 5-point scale.
Negative-review share
Reviews citing „Echtzeit", „Referenzkonto" or a competitor, trending down over two quarters.

Scope & honesty: all data is fake; no backend, no auth, no live prices, no real payments, no investment advice. The ETA is shown as an estimate; the exact credit at the receiving bank can't be guaranteed. This is phase 1 (transparency now); faster outbound payouts are the phase-2 engineering track.
